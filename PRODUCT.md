# FamiCare Product Context

## Users
- Families caring for elderly members.
- Caregivers tracking health data (blood pressure, heart rate, medications).
- Elderly members who interact via LINE.

## Brand & Tone
- **Tone**: Professional, clinical yet empathetic, trustworthy.
- **Aesthetic**: Modern Minimalist, "Clinical Premium".
- **Keywords**: Clean, Safe, Precise, Accessible.

## Strategic Principles
- **Clarity**: Health data must be unambiguous.
- **Trust**: Visuals must feel stable and secure.
- **Efficiency**: Quick actions for busy caregivers.

## Design System (DESIGN.md)
- **Primary**: #0ea5e9 (Medical Blue)
- **Secondary**: #003751 (Medical Navy)
- **Background**: #f8fafc (Medical BG)
- **Font**: Anuphan (Sans/Heading)
- **Radius**: 8px (Base), 16px (Card)
